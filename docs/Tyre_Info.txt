Typical tyre parameters for F1 tyres: (All values are based on optimal conditions)

Static friction peak (mu_s): 1.4-1.7 (Normal tyres: 0.8-0.9)
Kinematic friction (mu_k): 1.7-1.9 (Normal tyres: 0.7)
Peak slip angle (alpha_peak): 4-8 degrees (Normal tyres: 5-10 degrees)
Zero slip angle (alpha_zero): 2-4 degrees (Normal tyres: 5-10 degrees)
Curvature factor (C): 100-200 (Normal tyres: 100-200)
Slip Ratio (SR): 0.05-0.1 (Normal tyres: 0.1-0.25)
Reference speed (V0): 50-100 m/s (Normal tyres: 30-50 m/s)
