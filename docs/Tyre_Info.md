## Typical tyre parameters for F1 tyres: (All values are based on optimal conditions)
---
- **Static friction peak (mu_s):** F1: 1.4-1.7 (Normal tyres: 0.8-0.9)
- **Kinematic friction (mu_k):** F1: 1.7-1.9 (Normal tyres: 0.7)
- **Peak slip angle (alpha_peak):** F1: 4-8 degrees (Normal tyres: 5-10 degrees)
- **Zero slip angle (alpha_zero):** F1: 2-4 degrees (Normal tyres: 5-10 degrees)
- **Curvature factor (C):** F1: 100-200 (Normal tyres: 100-200)
- **Slip Ratio (SR):** F1: 0.05-0.1 (Normal tyres: 0.1-0.25)
- **Reference speed (V0):** F1: 50-100 m/s (Normal tyres: 30-50 m/s)
